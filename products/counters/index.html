Product Overview

Dr. Pharm not only specializes in solid dose manufacturing but also provides excellent tablet and capsule counting equipment. Start automating your packaging process with a tablet, capsule, pill counter today. Vibration tray channel technology enables several product types to be counted and inserted bottles of your specification. With individual counter channels and 3 separate tray units, full automation is acheieved and control streamlining your packaging process. Find out more by selecting one of the counter machine models, or give us a call today at 1-866-778-7826

Features:

  • Multi-Lane vibratory tray counters

  • Multiple levels of isolated vibration control

  • Dynamic Infrared Scanning Technology determines proper capsule, tablet, pill size that is being counted

  • Automatic rejection of incorrect count and bottles with damaged or broken capsules and tablets

  • Easy to disassemble and clean

  • 304 stainless steel construction
