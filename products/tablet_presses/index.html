Product Overview

Dr. Pharm offers a wide variety tablet pressing equipment. Every model is equipped with a pre-compression and main-press roller, both capable of multiple tons of applied force. All machines feature automatic weight control enabling full automatic tablet pressing with precise weight control. If you require a specific tablet shape whether it is round, irregular, embossed, debossed, or annular, Dr. Pharm offers quailty tooling for both TSM and EU standards.
