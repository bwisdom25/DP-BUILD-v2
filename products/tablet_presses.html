Product Overview

Dr. Pharm offers a wide variety tablet pressing equipment. Every model is equipped with a pre-compression and main-press roller, both capable of multiple tonnes or kilonewtons of applied pressure. All machines also feature automatic weight control allowing for full automatic tablet pressing with precise weight control. If you need a specific tablet shape whether it is round, irregular, embossed, debossed, or annular, Dr. Pharm offers the highest quailty tooling for both TSM and EU standards. Get more information by checking out one of the many tablet presses Dr. Pharm offers, Download a Brochure, or give us a call today at 1-866-778-7826

Features:

  • High precision tablet pressing technology

  • All tablet machines meet cGMP and FDA requirements

  • Equipped with multi tonne pre-compression and main compression rollers

  • Designed for easy disassembly and cleaning
