About Us

Dr. Pharm is a global manufacturer and supplier of precision machinery to the Pharmaceutical and Dietary Supplement Industries. Founded in 1986 under privatization programs, Dr. Pharm started establishing their mark as a leader in the industry. Dr. Pharm staffs over 600 employees many of which are skilled technicians with a large percentage being engineering graduates. Headquartered in a 300,000 ft2 facility in Beijing, Dr. Pharm utilizes several CNC machines, skilled fabricators, machinists, and enforces strict Quality Assurance and Quality Control protocols to manufacture quality equipment. Recently Dr. Pharm acquired SAR Labortecnic (Previously a division of Glatt Industries) expanding the product line to include Wet Granulators, Fluid Bed Drying, Tablet Coating, and Blending. To keep up with the increased demand and the additional product line, a new facility with over 900,000 ft2 under roof opened the doors in 2013. Dr. Pharm strives for continual growth and prides themselves as industry leaders in precision equipment.

Right Stuff Equipment is the exclusive North American representative for Dr. Pharm. All Dr. Pharm machines and parts are provided and serviced through Right Stuff Equipment. Beyond the partnership with Dr. Pharm, Right Stuff Equipment also provides engineering solutions for integrated automation equipment specialized in the food and beverage industry. Intricate automation line designs, custom built equipment, and full production line equipment are all designed and manufactured by Right Stuff Equipment. Located in beautiful Denver, Colorado, the 21,000 ft2 Right Stuff headquarters facility has a highly skilled staff of engineers along with state of the art fabrication capabilities.
