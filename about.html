About Us

Dr. Pharm is global manufactuerer and supplier of precision machinery to the Dietary and Supplemnt Industries. Founded in 1986 under priviatization programs within China, Dr. Pharm started establishing their mark as a leader in precise equipment manufacturing. Dr. Pharm staffs over 600 employees many of which are engineering graduates with excellent qualifications. Headquartered in a 180,000 sqaure foot facility in Beijing, China, Dr. Pharm utilizes several CNC machines, skilled fabricators and machinests, and strict Quality Assurance and Quality Contorl protocols to produce high quality equipment.Recently Dr. Pharm acquired SAR Labortecnic (Previously a division of Glatt Industries) expanding their product line to include Wet Granulators, Fluid Bed Drying, and Coating. To keep up with high demand and the new product line, a new facility with over 900,000 square feet under roof opened the doors in 2013. Dr. Pharm strives for continual growth and prides themselves as industry leaders in precision equipment.




Right Stuff Equipment is the exclusive North American representative for Dr. Pharm. All Dr. Pharm machines and parts are provided and serviced through Right Stuff Equipment. Beyond the partnership with Dr. Pharm, Right Stuff Equipment also provides engineering solutions for integrated automation equipment specialized in the food and beverage industry. Intricate automation line designs, custom built equipment, full production line equipment are all created and built with Right Stuff Equipment. Located in beautiful Denver, Colorado, the 21,000 square feet Right Stuff headquarters holds facility for a highly skilled staff of engineers along with state of the art fabrication machinery.
